About the Trust

The Shri Sai Baba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi, is the governing and administrative body of the Shri Sai Baba Temple. It was established to preserve and promote the teachings of Sai Baba and to manage the temple and associated services.

Surrounding Area

Shirdi, a holy town in Maharashtra, is known for the temple and nearby places like Dwarkamai, Chavadi, Gurusthan, and the Lendi Baug garden. Pilgrims can explore the spiritual heritage of the area.

Donations

Devotees can contribute to the trust online or offline. Donations support temple maintenance, education, health care, and free meals through the prasadalay.

Prasadalay

The prasadalay serves free meals to thousands of devotees every day. Timings and special offerings are displayed at the entrance and updated on this site.
